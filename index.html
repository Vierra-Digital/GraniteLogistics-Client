How it works

Four steps, fully tracked

  1. 01Order won

    Pulled automatically from your commerce backend the moment a sale or auction closes.

  2. 02Label & pickup

    A carrier-ready label prints; the runner captures a condition photo and bins the item.

  3. 03Manifest & ship

    Items are ZIP pre-sorted, batched to a carrier manifest, and assigned a dock lane.

  4. 04Delivery confirmed

    Carrier scans flow back in real time, closing with a final proof-of-delivery photo.

Chain of custody

Proof at every handoff

Granite holds a signed, time-stamped record for every package — the label it shipped on, who touched it, and photographic proof of its condition at pickup and delivery.
